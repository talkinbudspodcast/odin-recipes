Greek Fries

These restaurant quality fries can easily be whipped up using your air fryer and a few pantry staples!

Ingredients:

  • 8 medium-sized, waxy potatoes (Yukon Gold works best)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp homemade Greek seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup feta crumbles
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped curly parsley
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped red onion
  • 4 lemon wedges, for serving
  • optional: tzatziki, lemon dill sauce, or aioli, for dipping

How to make them:

  1. Slice potatoes into french fries.
  2. Toss in olive oil & Greek seasoning
  3. Cook in air fryer at 390F for about 30 mins, turning halfway.
  4. Serve with toppings and dipping sauce!
