Frank's Red Hot Buffalo Dip

This ooey gooey, spicy, cheesy dish is always a crowd-pleaser!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded chicken
  • 500g cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup Frank's Red Hot Sauce (add more or less based on your spice preference)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 bag Tostitos Scoops

How to make it:

  1. In a stand mixer, combine shredded chicken, cream cheese, ranch dressing, Frank's Red Hot Sauce, and 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese.
  2. Spread mixture evenly into an oven-safe dish.
  3. Sprinkle remaining 1/2 cup of shredded cheese to top of dish
  4. Bake at 350F for about 25 mins or until cheese on top is melted
  5. Serve immediately with Tostitos Scoops!
